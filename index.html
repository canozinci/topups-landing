Use Case: AI Content Generator

Pricing Model: Freemium + Tiered Credits

Before TopUps:

  • Free plan: 3 short articles/month, limited features
  • Basic plan: $29/month for 50 articles
  • Pro plan: $99/month for 200 articles
  • Unused articles don't roll over
  • No flexibility for high-volume months

With TopUps:

  • Free tier: 10 credits/month (1 credit = 1 short article)
  • Basic plan: $29/month for 60 credits
  • Pro plan: $99/month for 250 credits
  • Additional features cost extra credits
  • Unused credits roll over (max 3 months)
  • Purchase additional credit packs: 100 for $25, 500 for $100

Benefits:

  • Freemium model attracts new users and enables upselling
  • Credit system provides flexibility for varying content needs
  • Roll-over credits reduce waste and improve customer satisfaction
  • Additional credit packs allow for handling high-volume periods
  • Feature-based credit usage encourages exploration of advanced tools
